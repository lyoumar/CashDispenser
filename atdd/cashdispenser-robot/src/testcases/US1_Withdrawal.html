US1 – Successful Withdrawal

Description

Customer withdraws money by using a Cash dispenser. First they have to authenticate themselves and then specify how much should be withdrawn. Finally, the Cash Dispenser should return the requested amount of money and decrease the balance on the Customer's associated bank Account.
